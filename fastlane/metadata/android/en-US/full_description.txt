🌠 Diadem

Diadem is a next-gen GO Map. Use this app to connect to any Diadem Map covering your area.

Features:
• Connect to any Diadem Map
• Modern UI
• Great performance
• Easy to use
• 100% open-source

This app is purely a client, you will connect to a backend hosted by third-parties. They will be responsible for how they collect and store your data.
